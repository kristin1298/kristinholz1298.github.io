OUR SOLUTION
ASSISTANT RESCUE ORGANIZATIONS

Providing adequate help for victims of trafficaccidents has been becoming progressively harder for emergency helpers in the past few years, especially for firefighters. Today, firefighters in Germany still lack the possibility to quickly and easily get the information they need concerning a vehicular accident at hand. This poses a problem, since a quick and unproblematic rescue is paramount to ensure the victim’s well-being.

Why

We started out with a Design Thinking workshop and research on the emergency personnel and set our focus on the information flow. We worked in weekly sprints as 2 sub teams Design and Dev-elopment principally “together alone”. Main focus by UI / UX Design was, that the app is intuitive and that emergency personnel and call center operators are assisted with clear UI in stress situations.
