Assistant Rescue Organisation is a functioning concept app that improves communication of crucial information for emergency personnel in case of reported car crashes.
Our interactive design solution “ARO” came into life as part of our major course Inter-action Design at the Mannheim University of Applied Sciences. The challenge about this project was, that we had to work with a real client. Our team consisted of 4 Design students and 5 Computer Science students.

The "Digital Waitingroom Project" is an application that makes it easier for users to book a doctor's appointment. With the help of a few clicks, they can choose a suitable appointment and also set a reminder. Therefore, the user is reminded in time of his appointment and, if necessary, also informed if delays may occur. In this way, the user does not have to wait in the medical practice and can spend the waiting time reasonably.

The UI UX Guide is a collection of user interface and user experience guidelines and serves as an aid to understanding and correctly applying the design process, as well as the most important design guidelines, in order to provide the user with an optimal user experience. The guide is primarily aimed at beginners in user interface and user experience design. It gives you the opportunity to learn basic guidelines and how to apply them with the help of examples. It is also suitable for experienced UI UX designers who want to refresh their knowledge of the various guidelines.
